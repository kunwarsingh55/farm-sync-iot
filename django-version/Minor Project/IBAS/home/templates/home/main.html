    IoT and Farming

    IoT Based Agricultural Supervision is an implementation of various devices and technologies like Internet, Cloud, IoT and AI to control, observe or automatically manage farming based tasks like keeping moisture levels of the soil in check , keeping track of pH level in the soil and alarm the farmer if levels become harmful for the crops.
